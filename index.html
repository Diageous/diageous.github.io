About


You have more to say with your art. Whether it's music, photography, writing, speaking or any other creative area, it's our firm stance that you can do much more than just entertain - you have to ability to make an impact doing what you love. We want to help you do it!

Our Vision

1. Art is about a community, rather than an individual. Leave it to big corporations to sell things we don't need - most artists don't have a huge following, so collaboration needs to come before capital. We are all learners, and at the same time we all have things to share.

2. Art is both convergent and divergent. We create based on our experiences and influences, but we incorporate our own unique personality to make it our own. We rely on other artists to get us going, but we ultimately strive to find ourselves creatively.

3. Art should encourage other art. Good art will inspire others to create and encourage emmulation. We create because we have something to say and we have found others using their art to say things words cannot.

4. Art is uniquely human. Thus, it is a product of human thought, dignity, and emotion. (Read G.K. Chesterton's The Everlasting Man for a deeper look at this)

5. Art is a channel for cultural change. When we look at art and how themes have changed over the years, there's a relation to social awareness and current events. Art is not a silent bystander, and it has power to make a difference.

About Our Name
